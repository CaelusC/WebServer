Houses of the Realm

Three major factions shape this new world.

House of Adamant

Systematic Takeover & Optimization of Natural Extractives

These are STONE.corp's industrial colonists. Ruthless, structured, and all about profit.

Hard Monopolies:

  • 'Create' & 'Immersive Engineering' mods
  • Rail systems and Redstone automation

Soft Monopolies:

  • Mining, digging and excavation
  • Transportation of items and entities
Read Constitution

House of Theurgy

Witches and Warlocks of the Covenant Academy. Research-obsessed, dangerous, and not entirely sane.

Hard Monopolies:

  • 'Ars Nouveau' & 'Apotheosis' mods
  • 'Modular Golems' mod
  • Hostile mob farms

Soft Monopolies:

  • Magical resource gathering
  • Dungeon raids and exploring dimensions
Read Constitution

House of Demeter

Peaceful but cult-like agriculturalists. Sent by Tzar of Leria, they value harmony and community.

Hard Monopolies:

  • 'Villager Recruits & Workers' mods
  • 'Farmer's Delight' & food mods
  • Farming and Herbalism

Soft Monopolies:

  • Breeding and livestock
  • Potion & Wine making
Read Constitution
