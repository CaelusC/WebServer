Houses of the Realm

Three major factions shape this new world. Learn their ways, join their cause, and read their constitutions.
You are also obligated to Follow their Monopolies:
Hard Monopoly - Only they are allowed to do this.
Soft Monopoly - You should rely on them for this, but you are also allowed to do it.

House of Adamant

Systematic
Takeover &
Optimization of
Natural
Extractives

These are STONE.corp's industrial colonists. Ruthless, structured, and all about profit. One Leader. Three subordinates. They came to the New World for one reason and one reason only, extract all.

Hard Monopolies:

  • 'Create' mod machines
  • Rail systems and Redstone Automation

Soft Monopolies:

  • Mining and excavation
  • Transportation

House of Theurgy

Witches and Warlocks of the Covenant Academy. Research-obsessed, dangerous, and not entirely sane. They serve no gods, only Curiosity and Knowledge. They came to the New World because of rumours of Ancient Magical Artifacts, they are willing to kill anything for these Artifacts.

Hard Monopolies:

  • All magical. (Other Houses can use only with a permit from Theurgy)
  • Hostile mob farms

Soft Monopolies:

  • Mystical resource gathering
  • Dungeon Raids, Rituals and exploring other dimensions

House of Demeter

Peaceful but cult-like agriculturalists. Sent by Tzar of Leria, they value harmony, seasonal rituals, and community. They have traveled to the New World to protect nature and help it thrive, in an ever expanding Empire. Some Call them a Cult, but they prefer 'Peaceful Community for like-minded People'

Hard Monopolies:

  • 'Villager Recruits & Workers' mods
  • Farmer's Delight food systems

Soft Monopolies:

  • Breeding and livestock
  • Crops and Herbalism
