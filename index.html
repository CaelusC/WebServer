The Helianthus Server

The world of Helianthus is ruled by a merciless Sun God. His light brings ruin, not warmth. Crops wither, rivers dry, and those who can't adapt don't last long.

The Server isn’t built for loners. No players scattered in quiet bases. Everything revolves around the Three Ancient Houses: Adamant, Theurgy, and Demeter. Old powers with territory, history, and grudges to protect.

You join a House to survive. Each has its own land, role, and way of enduring Helianthus. Your story and your obligations start there. Act like your House!!!

House regions mean safety and resources. Expanding takes people, strategy, and Coin. What your House has, others want. What you don’t, you trade for. Nothing’s free.

Each House specializes in something. Farming, Magic, Machinery, Alchemy, Management. No House can do it all. You must rely on others, whether you like it or not. Progress and setbacks are shared.

Build walls and protect yourself, outside the walls, the world is unbearable. Stronger mobs. Harsher weather. Food runs low. Vanilla strategies won’t save you. Mistakes cost gear, time, and lives. But deeper out, things stir old, buried, dangerous, and maybe worth the risk.

Pick a House. Take your place. Learn to survive. Helianthus doesn’t care who you are. Worship beneath him, and maybe his light spares you one more day.
