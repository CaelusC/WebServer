Changes Coming Next Season

The upcoming Season will be a hard reset. Refined systems, better performance. See some of the changes below:

1. Hosting & Performance Upgrade

The server is getting a huge hardware boost:

  • CPU: 6 vCores @ 5.7 GHz
  • RAM: 22 GB DDR5
  • Storage: 350 GB NVMe SSD
  • Ports: 10 allocated

TLDR: better performance and room for heavier mods and events.

2. Lore – The Will of Helianthus

Last cycle was… disappointing.

Helianthus found the world stale and weak, so he reshaped it entirely:

  • More Dungeons.
  • More Creatures.
  • Bloodthirsty Demigods at their Thrones.

He also created three lesser gods:

  • Scythia: Goddess of Punishment
  • Leonidas: God of Conflict
  • Bremen: God of Expiration

You can now perform deeds for these gods in exchange for divine gifts. Loyalty matters.

3. Divine Events

Each lesser god will host one major event this season (3 total).

Houses compete.

Winners gain a divine favor.

Rewards can be a powerful gift or a divine deed.

Not cosmetic. They will affect the power of Houses.

4. World Generation Overhaul

We're switching terrain systems:

Removed: Biomes O' Plenty

Added:

  • Terralith
  • Regions Unexplored
  • Geolosys
  • And others...

Expect a more exploration-heavy and dangerous world.

5. Magic System Changes (House of Theurgy)

Theurgy magic is shifting direction:

Removed: Ars Nouveau

Added: Iron's Spells & Spellbooks

More direct, combat-oriented magic. More raw power and branching into different magic classes.

6. House Constitutions Updated

Each House will receive constitution changes aimed at:

  • Easier onboarding.
  • Clearer cooperation rules.
  • Less friction between House Deals.

Details will be published per House before the season starts.

Final Notes

This season is designed to be:

  • More alive.
  • More consequential.
  • More fun to explore.
  • Hopefully on a bigger scale.

More detailed breakdowns (Events, Constitutions, Lore) will follow.
