The Helianthus Server

The world of Helianthus is ruled by a merciless Sun God. His light brings ruin, not warmth. Crops wither, rivers dry, and those who can't adapt don't last long.

A world that isn't built for solitude or surviving alone. You won’t find players scattered across the map building quiet bases. Life here is shaped by the Three Ancient Houses: Adamant, Theurgy and Demeter. Powerful factions with history, grudges, and territory to protect.

When you join, you become part of a House. For survival. Each House has its own land, purpose, and way of enduring Helianthus. Your story begins there. So do your obligations, and act like your House.

Territory means safety. It means access to resources. Expanding territory takes people, strategy, and Coin. What your House controls, others will want. What you lack, you'll need to trade for from other Houses. Nothing is free here.

Each House specializes in specific resource. Farming, Magic, Machinery, Mining, Alchemy, Management. No House can do everything. You will rely on others, whether you like it or not. Progress is shared. So are setbacks.

The outside world is worse. The mobs are stronger. The weather is brutal. Food runs out. The systems you know from vanilla won't save you. Mistakes cost time, gear, and lives. But if you push deeper, you might find things buried long ago. Some of them useful. Some better left alone.

Pick a House. Take your place. Learn to survive. Helianthus doesn’t care who you are. As long as you worship beneath him, his rays might spare you another day.
