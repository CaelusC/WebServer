Birth of Helianthus

From the Dark Pits, a little drop of light pushed out. Neither god nor beast, it radiates warmth like a dying star. It speaks in tongues. It dreams in clouds. And it watches. From its birth it has enjoyed suffering, and now it will enjoy your suffering. Easily upset, you best worship it or suffer its wrath.

The Three Houses

The world is pulled in different directions by many powers. Among them, three nations stand out the most.

Leria is ruled by a Tzar and his family. They sent to the New World the people now known as House Demeter to spread their religion and their philosophy of agricultural dominion.

The Covenant is a network of Academies spread across the world, bound together by their hunger for power. Ruled by the Supreme Arch Mage, they sent a group now known as House Theurgy to the New World to hunt down magical artifacts and knowledge and bring it back.

Then there's STONE.corp. A massive company stretching across multiple regions with one goal and one goal only. Strip the earth of every last resource and turn it into profit. They sent a team now called House Adamant to do exactly that in the New World.

The Eternal Siege

The mobs have changed. No longer beasts. No longer hollow. Helianthus, in his unknowable will, breathed consciousness into their empty forms. A cruel gift. Now they think. They speak in silence. They move with purpose. Packs swarm like wolves, flank close in like soldiers. One distracts. One surrounds. One kills.

They do not sleep. They do not retreat. Their only reason to exist is to tear you into pieces.

Player Role

You arrive in the dark with a gift from Helianthus. Your task: choose a House and claim relics. But most importantly... Hold Dominion over the New World, only one House can truly win.
