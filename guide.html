Guide to the Realm

Installing the Modpack

Required: Modrinth App and our Official Modpack

  1. Download our Official Modpack from Discord (Full or Bare Bones)
  2. Install Modrinth Launcher from modrinth.com/app
  3. Click the plus icon, then "From File"
  4. Choose the Modpack file you downloaded
  5. Wait for install and press Play
  6. Add your username in the Whitelist Discord channel

Having trouble? Join our Discord!

Living as Your House

  • Pick your House carefully - you CANNOT switch later
  • Find your House Territory and meet your Housemates
  • Learn the Lore and Monopolies your House owns
  • Support your House economy and claim territory

Common Questions

How do I find my House's territory?

Check your House's Discord Channel for pinned Coordinates.

Can I switch Houses?

No! Pick carefully - it's permanent.

How do I trade with other Houses?

Use the central marketplace or Discord. All trades must be made with Coin.
