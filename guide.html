Guide to the Realm

Installing the Modpack

Required: Modrinth App and our Official Modpack
  • Helianthus [Full Modpack]
    Helianthus [Bare Bones]
  • Download our Official Modpack from the links above. There are two options: Full Modpack with extra visuals and Shaders already setup, Or Bare Bones where only the needed mods for the server are included. Click the three dots on the right side of the screen and then click "Download"
  • Install Modrinth Launcher from their official website. [Modrinth Website]
  • (Inside of Modrinth App) Click on the big plus icon "Creating an instance" then click on "From File".
  • And Choose the Modpack file you just downloaded.
  • Wait for it to fully Install and press "Play"
  • Add your username in the Whitelist Discord channel
Having trouble? Join our Discord! We’ll help you out.

Living as Your House

  • Pick your House carefully, you CANNOT switch later.
  • Find your House Territory and meet your Housemates.
  • Use your Quest Book to follow your House’s path.
  • Learn the Lore and the Monopolies your House owns. And follow them!
  • Support your House economy and claim territory.
Your House is your family — help them thrive!

Common Questions

  • How do I find my House's territory?
    Check your Houses Discord Channel, there should be pinned Coordinates. Each House starts with NPCs and clear direction.
  • Can I switch Houses?
    Not mid-season. Pick carefully... it's a permanent choice for now.
  • How do I trade with other Houses?
    Use the central marketplace or arrange trades on Discord. All trades must be made with Coin. You can get Coins at spawn, from NPC's or from each other.
  • What happens if I die?
    We have a revive mod. Work together! You can explore together and if one of you gets killed, the others can pick you up in a certain timespan.
  • When does the season end?
    This Season will end in 4 months or more depending on how alive the server is.
  • Can I level up other skills that aren't my House-specific?
    Yes, you can do the bare minimum leveling for everything. But before you can level up More you need to Max out your House-specific Skills first! Do Not just level up everything. That defeats the Points of Houses!!
