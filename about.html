About Our Community

We're a small but dedicated group of Minecraft players who wanted something more... more lore, more immersion, more difficulty. We work on this server between our studies and jobs, so things might go slow sometimes, but every detail has love behind it.

We believe in Fair Play, Hard Survival, Roleplay, and creating something actually unique. Whether you're a builder, hardcore player, or someone who just likes to be part of a community, we probably have a House for you!
