About Our Community

We're a small but dedicated group of Minecraft players who wanted something more.. more lore, more immersion, more structure. We work on this server between our studies and jobs, so things might go slow sometimes, but every detail has love behind it.

We believe in fair play, weird roleplay, and creating something actually unique. Whether you're a builder, hardcore min-maxer, or someone who just likes watching the world burn, we probably have a House for you.
