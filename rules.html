⚖️ Rules of the Helianthus Realm ⚖️

General Conduct

  • No griefing or stealing. Don’t enter, loot, or modify builds that aren't yours unless you’ve been given clear permission.
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, racism, homophobia or any other toxicity.
  • No hacks, cheats, or exploits... including X-ray or duping.
  • Killing other players is only allowed by agreement, war, paid assassination or RP event.
  • Do not use House or shared resources without permission.
  • If you’re causing constant friction with others, even without ‘breaking a rule’, you may still be kicked.
  • Attempting to ignore the rules via technicalities, gaslighting, or pretending ignorance will be treated the same as breaking them!

Building Rules

  • Buildings must match your House theme and terrain. No floating messes or random spam structures.
  • You must build within the given Region, And near your House HQ.
  • 'Large' House projects can only be done with your Houses elected Leader's approval.
  • If you abandon a project, tell your House leader so it can be repurposed or removed.

Monopoly System

  • Hard Monopoly - exclusive rights. Only the owning House may operate.
  • Soft Monopoly - others may participate, but must rely on the owner House as the main provider.
  • Violating a Hard Monopoly without a permit will result in confiscation, destruction of the illegal infrastructure, and fines or raids.
  • You can produce things yourself, but if you’re undercutting or bypassing the monopoly owner deliberately, that’s smuggling.
  • Breaking monopoly/smuggling will result in RP or administrative consequences.

Roleplay & Community

  • Maintain in-character behavior where appropriate.
  • Conflict is allowed in RP, but real toxicity is not.
  • Trade, politics, and alliances are encouraged, keep it fun and fair.
  • Do Not trade for unreasonable deals, they cannot be unreasonable to you.

Claiming Wars

  • Each Region has a name and Coin price. See Region Prices in 'Lore' page
  • Only Houses (not individuals) can bid on unclaimed Regions.
  • The highest bidder wins and must have the Coin at the time of bidding.
  • The first Region a House wins becomes their Official Region, which cannot be stolen.

Stealing Regions

  • Claimed Regions (excluding Official Regions) can be stolen by other Houses.
  • Each Region has a Tower with a colored flag representing the current owner.
  • To steal a Region:

    - The House Ruler must initiate the raid.

    - At least 4 House members must participate.

    - The raid must be announced to the Admins prior (Admins will broadcast it Beginning).

  • During the raid, change the flag to your House color and hold it for 6 real-time hours.
  • The flag must remain intact and not be recaptured during this time.
  • You can build and fortify the Tower as you wish.
  • Combat is only allowed within 30 blocks of the Tower during the raid.

Stealing Cooldowns & Timing

  • Each House may only attempt one steal per week.

Final Notes

  • Unsure about something? Ask an admin or House leader.
  • Rules may evolve... stay updated through server announcements.
  • This world is shared. Respect the story we're building together.
